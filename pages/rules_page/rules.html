Rules Page

Step 1: Game Setup:

Cards will be randomly generated throughout the game board.

Step 2: Playing the game

Each player will click 2 cards at a time to reveal the image.

Step 3: Match the cards

If they match, they remain face-up. If someone matches 2, they get an extra turn. Otherwise, the other player gets a turn.

Step 4: Repeat

Repeat steps 2 and 3 until all cards are face-up.

Step 5: Winning

The person who has the higher score of the current game wins, unless the scores are even then nobody wins.
